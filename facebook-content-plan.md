# trace.memorial — Facebook Content Plan
## Daily launch series: 2 June – 3 July 2026 (32 posts)

---

## Audience

Grieving pet owners. People who have recently lost a pet, or who know someone who has.
Not counsellors — that is the Academy's audience. This page speaks directly to the person
who is missing their animal.

Tone: warm, intimate, unhurried. No grief exploitation. No pressure. The early access offer
is a gentle nudge, not a hard sell.

Join as yourself first, not as the page. Spend a week reading and responding with genuine
support before mentioning trace.memorial. When you do share, frame it as a resource.

## Notes on tone

- Write as someone who understands the grief, not as a service provider
- No urgency language in the groups — only on the page itself
- Do not use the word "product" or "service" in group posts
- Share memorial page examples only with explicit permission from the families involved
  (Bella, Pepper, Willow etc. are sample pages — confirm with Marta before using publicly)
- Urgency language escalates gradually: "free for the first 100" (early) to "a few spaces left" (mid) to "last few spaces" (final week)
